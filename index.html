What is Stucardent?

Stucardent stores your cards in one place! Transport and student cards are in one place and never get lost. Everyone can use it! It is free! If you have questions contact us!

Usable on public transport

Stucardent can be used on public transport. Stored cards can be activated for use on public transport. Just press “activate” and your ready to go!

All cards in one place

Your cards will not be lost! Cards are safely stored in this app - all in one place!

Completely free

Your cards are stored here and usable in public transport! All for free!
